Our Promise

From Our River to Your Home

At Twomeals Ventures, we believe pets deserve real food. Our journey begins at the Nam Ngum River in Laos, where we sustainably breed our own Tilapia in river pens.

We use a precise low-temperature dehydration process. This takes longer than baking, but it ensures that the natural vitamins and minerals of the fish are preserved. The result is a snack that is not only delicious but also highly nutritious.
